# What dynamic metrics are available for my country?  (cont.)

![How to Find a Country](images/gifs/9_dynamic_metrics2.gif)

- Click on the ![](images/icons/info-icon.png) icon to view layer info, the info pages provide a brief description of the data, related paper to read and source links.
- To download summary data for the metric in .csv or .json format, click on the arrow icon ![](images/icons/download-icon.png). You can also download the data from source links on the layer's info pages.
