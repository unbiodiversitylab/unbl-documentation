# How do I adjust my map view?

There are several features that can assist you to navigate the map screen. These include:

1. *Move map*: Use your mouse to click and drag the portion of the map you would like to view into the middle of the screen.

2. *Zoom in/out:* Click on the +/- icons at the bottom right of the map or use the scrolling wheel on your mouse. 

3. *Center place:* Click on the center place button above +/-. If you have selected a place in the left menu bar, this will re-center the map over the selected place.

4. *Hide left menu bar:* Click on the arrow at the top of the left menu to collapse the dataset panel for a larger map view. Click again to expand the panel.
