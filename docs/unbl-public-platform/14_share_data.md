Sharing datasets which are available on the public platform is easy. To do so:

1.	Activate the dataset(s) of interest to your map view.

2.	Copy the URL displayed in your browser.

3.	Share! Any datasets on our public platform will be accessible by anyone, regardless of whether they are a registered user.
