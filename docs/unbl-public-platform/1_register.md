# How do I register or log-in?

Before you begin exploring the maps, register for the UN Biodiversity Lab.

1. Click the 'Launch map' page of the [UN Biodiversity Lab](https://unbiodiversitylab.org/) website.

	![](images/en/image003.png)

2. Once this has loaded, select the account icon in the top right-hand corner and choose 'sign in'. In the loaded page then choose 'sign up'. Enter your email, set your password, name, country, institution/organization, and organization type to sign up.

	![](images/en/image005.png)

	![](images/en/image007.png)

3. You will receive an email within a few minutes. Follow the instructions in this email to verify your account. 

	![](images/en/image009.png)

4. Once your account is verified, you can log in using your email address and password each time you access the platform.

	![](images/en/image011.png)

5. You can log out at any time by clicking on your user icon and selecting 'Sign Out'.

	![](images/en/image013.png)
	![](images/en/image015.png)
