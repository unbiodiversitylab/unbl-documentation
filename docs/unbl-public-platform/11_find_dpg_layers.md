# How do I find the Digital Public Good (DPG) open data layers?

To find the Digital Public Good (DPG) open data layers in UNBL, follow these steps:

![Find DPG layers](images/gifs/10_find_layers.gif)


1. Click on the *LAYERS* icon.
2. Click to expand the filters box.
3. Click to expand the Layer tags, you then can select the layer tag and layer category of interest.
4. Click again the filter icon to visualize the complete list of layers that belong to your selected category, you then can select layer of interest from the layer result list.
5. Click the toggle to the left of the layer name to load this layer to the map.
6. Click the toggle again or click the *X* icon on the layer info to remove this layer.
